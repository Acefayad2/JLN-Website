Frequently Asked Questions

What types of projects does JLN Construction handle?

We handle a wide range of projects including healthcare facilities, educational institutions, commercial buildings, roads, bridges, drainage systems, and environmental management projects.

Do you work internationally?

Yes, we operate in both Liberia and the United States, serving clients across West Africa and beyond.

What is your typical project timeline?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. We work closely with clients to establish realistic schedules and ensure on-time delivery.

Do you provide post-construction support?

Absolutely. We offer comprehensive post-construction support including follow-ups, modifications, maintenance guidance, and ongoing communication.

What safety standards do you follow?

We adhere to international safety standards and implement comprehensive EHS protocols to protect workers, communities, and the environment.

How do you ensure quality control?

We implement rigorous QA/QC processes throughout all project phases, including regular inspections, testing, and continuous monitoring.
